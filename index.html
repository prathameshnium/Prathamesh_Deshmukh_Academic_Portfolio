Research Profile

Focusing on the intersection of magnetism and electricity in novel materials.

Core Research: Multiferroic Composites

My research is centered on fabricating and understanding materials where magnetic and electric properties are intrinsically linked. The goal is to contribute to next-generation sensors, memory devices, and energy-efficient electronics.

Key Activities

  • Novel material synthesis (e.g., BaFe10Sc2O19:KNbO3).
  • Design of custom probes for cryogenic measurements.
  • Structural analysis using Neutron & X-Ray Diffraction.
  • Characterization of magnetic and electric properties.
